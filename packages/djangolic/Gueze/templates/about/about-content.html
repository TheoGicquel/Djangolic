About Djangolic

Beer Database .

Djangolic is a beer CRUD (create, read, update, delete) website that allows users to easily create, view, update, and delete information about different beers.

The website has a user-friendly interface that allows users to easily browse through the various beers and their information, such as the name of the beer, the style of beer, the alcohol content, and other details.

Users can create a new beer by entering in the relevant information, and they can also update or delete existing entries as needed.

Djangolic is a great resource for anyone who loves beer and wants to keep track of their favorites, or for anyone who wants to learn more about different types of beer and their flavors and characteristics.
